Substance Use Support

This space is here to honor your journey. Whether you're exploring sobriety, harm reduction, or simply seeking clarity, you are welcome here. Healing is not a straight line — and you don’t have to walk it alone.

“Recovery is not about perfection. It’s about intention, compassion, and showing up for yourself.”

Gentle Tools for Support

  • Sobriety Journal – Track reflections, triggers, and wins
  • Harm Reduction Guide – Practical safety tips and emotional pacing
  • Support Groups – Find community and shared understanding

Affirmations for Recovery

  • I am allowed to take small steps.
  • My healing does not need to look like anyone else’s.
  • I am worthy of care, even when I struggle.

Need Immediate Help?

If you're in crisis or need urgent support, please reach out to a local mental health professional or helpline. You are not alone.

  • Kenya Mental Health Helpline: 1199 (Free, 24/7)
  • NACADA Kenya: nacada.go.ke
